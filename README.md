# MSc Thesis: SEO and SME Competitiveness in Digital Markets

**Author:** Jenil Arvindbhai Paladiya  
**Program:** Master of Science in Computer Science  
**University:** IU International University of Applied Sciences  
**Date:** March 2026  

## Thesis Title

**The Role of Search Engine Optimization (SEO) in Enhancing the Competitiveness of Small and Medium-Sized Enterprises (SMEs) in Digital Markets**

## Overview

This thesis examines how small and medium-sized enterprises use Search Engine Optimization as a digital marketing strategy to improve online visibility, customer acquisition, competitiveness, and firm performance.

The study is based on the Technology Acceptance Model and the digital capability perspective. It uses a quantitative research design with survey data from 150 SME owners and marketing managers.

## Key Topics

- Search Engine Optimization
- Digital Marketing
- SME Competitiveness
- Technology Acceptance Model
- Digital Marketing Capability
- Firm Performance
- Customer Acquisition

## Main Findings

The research indicates that SEO positively contributes to SME online visibility, customer acquisition, brand credibility, and competitiveness. It also highlights that content creation and keyword optimization are commonly used SEO practices, while technical SEO and backlink building are less adopted due to knowledge and resource limitations.
